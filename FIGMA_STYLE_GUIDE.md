# Figma Style Guide - Aliagui / ADINKRA

## Fonts

Use Google Fonts:

- Playfair Display: titles and product names
- Montserrat: body text, navigation, buttons, filters, footer

Recommended weights:

- Playfair Display: 500, 600, 700
- Montserrat: 400, 500, 600, 700

Google Fonts URLs:

- https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Playfair+Display
- https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Montserrat

## Motion / Interaction

Hover states:

- Nav links move up 1 px and darken
- Product card moves up 8 px
- Product image moves up 3 px and receives soft shadow
- Product title and price turn #934938 on hover
- Footer links move right 4 px and turn #FFDEA5
- Buttons move up 2 px and darken to #223153

Checkbox:

- Size: 20 px x 20 px
- Border: 1 px #75777E
- Radius: 4 px
- Checked background: #182442
- Checked label weight: 500
